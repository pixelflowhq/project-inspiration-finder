**Cell — Terminal spreadsheet editor with Vim keybindings**
A Rust-built, keyboard-driven spreadsheet that lives entirely in your terminal. Small, sharp tool — exactly the kind of side project that finds a devoted niche audience.

**Waiting Game — Mini-game component for LLM loading states**
