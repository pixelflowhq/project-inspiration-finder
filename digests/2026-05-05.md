# Daily Inspiration Digest — 2026-05-05

## AI/ML

**Muesli – Open-Source On-Device Meeting Transcription**
An open-source, privacy-first alternative to Granola and Wisprflow that transcribes meetings entirely on your device.
Worth watching because the cloud-to-local shift in AI tooling is accelerating, and meeting audio is deeply personal data.
[freedspeech.xyz](https://freedspeech.xyz)

**Bonsai 1.7B – Ternary Model at 442 Tokens/Sec on M4 Max**
A tiny ternary-quantized language model that screams on Apple Silicon, hitting 442 T/s with just 1.7B parameters.
This is the frontier of making local LLMs feel instant — real-time agent loops become practical at this speed.
[agents2agents.ai/bonsai](https://agents2agents.ai/bonsai)

**Ruflo – Multi-Agent Orchestration for Claude**
An agent orchestration platform that deploys Claude-powered swarms with RAG integration and self-learning capabilities.
If you want to experiment with multi-agent architectures without wiring everything yourself, this is a solid starting scaffold.
[github.com/ruvnet/ruflo](https://github.com/ruvnet/ruflo)

**TradingAgents – Multi-Agent LLM Framework for Finance**
A collaborative multi-agent system where specialized LLM agents handle research, analysis, and trade execution together.
Financial trading is one of the few domains where multi-agent coordination has a clear, measurable payoff signal.
[github.com/TauricResearch/TradingAgents](https://github.com/TauricResearch/TradingAgents)

**Manex – Persistent Memory for AI Conversations**
Captures and preserves the useful outputs from AI chats — answers, corrections, context — so they survive beyond the session.
This scratches a real itch; the ephemeral nature of AI conversations is a genuine productivity leak.

**Regulus by Cumbuca – AI Chatbot for Brazilian Banking Regulations**
An LLM trained on Brazil's Central Bank regulatory corpus to answer compliance questions instantly.
Niche regulatory RAG bots are an underexplored goldmine — every country and industry has its own version of this problem.

**Intuned Agent – Self-Healing Browser Automation**
An AI platform that builds and automatically maintains browser automation workflows when websites change underneath them.
Brittle scrapers are every developer's nemesis; AI-maintained selectors could finally break that cycle.
