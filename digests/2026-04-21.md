**QA Crow** — Automated bug backlog triage and prioritization.
Small scope, high impact — every team has a 500-item bug backlog nobody wants to sort. Buildable in 1–2 weeks.

**Holos** — Docker Compose-style YAML for QEMU/KVM VMs with GPU passthrough and health checks.
